About Me

Hi, I'm Brandon Ting — a passionate Unity Developer and Computer Science student at the University of Southampton Malaysia (First-Class Honours). I specialize in creating interactive games, simulations, and AR/VR experiences using Unity and C#. My development journey began with curiosity and has grown into a strong technical foundation across both gameplay programming and user experience design.

I've built and published multiple Unity projects, including a glowing arcade game that won the CSSC Game Expo 2024 and a full suite of mini-games through Unity's Junior Programmer Pathway. I also experiment with immersive tech, developing prototypes for VR rooms and AR face filters. Every project I take on is independently created — from ideation to coding, debugging, and final release.

Beyond game development, I’ve worked as a part-time coding instructor, teaching Python, Arduino, and Unity basics to students. I believe in continuous learning, creativity, and sharing knowledge with others. When I’m not coding, I enjoy collaborating with peers through the Computer Science Club and exploring digital art.
